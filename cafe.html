The experience

Three rooms, one long morning.

01 · The taste bar

Every drink we make, poured to order.

Sit at the bar and work through the range — espresso and slow-pour coffee, loose-leaf tea, and cold-pressed juice. Our team talks you through each one and builds a tasting flight around what you like. It's the fastest way to find your everyday cup.

Browse the coffee range
02 · The showroom

Get your hands on the whole line.

The full Atelier range is set up and running. Pull your own shot, watch the machine learn a profile, and ask the questions a spec sheet can't answer. When you're ready, we'll help you pick the one that fits your kitchen — or arrange a home delivery.

Explore the machines
03 · The table

Learn something, meet someone.

Most evenings the long table hosts something — a brewing class, a latte-art lab, a cupping, or just a slow community morning. Classes are small and hands-on, and most are free with a drink. Check what's on below and save a seat.
